# DMCA Takedown Process

## 1. Overview

Fathuss respects intellectual property rights and responds to valid Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) notices.

## 3. DMCA Notice Requirements

To submit a valid DMCA takedown notice, please provide the following information:

### 3.1 Identification of Infringing Material
- Specific URL or location of allegedly infringing content
- Description of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed
- Description of the allegedly infringing material

### 3.2 Ownership Information
- Your name, address, telephone number, and email address
- A statement that you have a good faith belief that use of the material is not authorized
- A statement that the information in the notice is accurate
- A statement, under penalty of perjury, that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner

### 3.3 Physical or Electronic Signature
- Physical signature or electronic equivalent

## 4. Counter-Notification Process

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notification:

### 4.1 Required Information
- Your name, address, telephone number, and email address
- Identification of the material removed and its location before removal
- A statement under penalty of perjury that the material was removed by mistake or misidentification
- A statement that you consent to the jurisdiction of the federal court in your district
- Physical or electronic signature

### 4.2 Counter-Notification Submission
Send to the DMCA Agent listed above.

## 5. Processing Timeline

### 5.1 Initial Review
- DMCA notices reviewed within 24-48 hours
- Validity assessment based on completeness and accuracy

### 5.2 Content Removal
- Valid notices: Content removed within 5 business days
- User notification sent regarding removal

### 5.3 Counter-Notification Handling
- Upon receipt of counter-notification, original complainant notified
- Content may be restored within 10-14 business days unless legal action initiated

## 6. Repeat Infringer Policy

### 6.1 Tracking
- Multiple DMCA violations tracked per user account
- Pattern of infringement documented

### 6.2 Consequences
- First violation: Warning and content removal
- Second violation: Temporary account suspension (30 days)
- Third violation: Permanent account termination

## 7. Good Faith Requirements

### 7.1 Notice Accuracy
- Notices must be truthful and not made for improper purposes
- Misrepresentation may result in liability for damages

### 7.2 Content Ownership
- Only copyright owners or authorized representatives may submit notices
- Verification of ownership may be requested

## 8. Educational Content and Fair Use

### 8.1 Academic Exceptions
- Code snippets for educational purposes may be protected under fair use
- Context and purpose considered in review process

### 8.2 Fair Use Assessment
- Transformative use, amount used, and market impact evaluated
- Educational platform status considered

## 9. International Copyright Considerations

### 9.1 Berne Convention
- Automatic copyright protection for foreign works
- No registration required for protection

### 9.2 Cross-Border Issues
- Cooperation with international copyright authorities
- Compliance with local laws in user's jurisdiction

## 10. Appeals Process

### 10.1 Internal Appeal
- Users may appeal DMCA decisions within 30 days
- Appeal reviewed by legal team and platform moderators

### 10.2 External Resolution
- Disputes not resolved internally may require legal action
- Court orders respected and implemented

## 11. Transparency and Reporting

### 11.1 Public Reporting
- Annual DMCA transparency report published
- Statistics on notices received and processed

### 11.2 Record Keeping
- All DMCA notices and responses archived for 3 years
- Audit trail maintained for compliance
