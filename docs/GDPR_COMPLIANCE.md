# GDPR Compliance Guidelines

## 1. Introduction

Fathuss is committed to complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and protecting user privacy rights.

## 2. Data Controller Responsibilities

### 2.1 Lawful Basis for Processing
- Consent: For optional data collection
- Contract: For account and service provision
- Legitimate Interest: For platform improvement and security
- Legal Obligation: For compliance with laws

### 2.2 Data Protection Officer
- Contact: privacy@fathuss.com
- Responsibilities: Oversee GDPR compliance, handle data subject requests

## 3. User Rights Under GDPR

### 3.1 Right to Access
Users can request a copy of their personal data held by the platform.

### 3.2 Right to Rectification
Users can correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

### 3.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
Users can request deletion of their personal data, subject to legal limitations.

### 3.4 Right to Data Portability
Users can request their data in a structured, machine-readable format.

### 3.5 Right to Object
Users can object to processing based on legitimate interests.

### 3.6 Right to Restrict Processing
Users can request limitation of data processing in certain circumstances.

## 4. Data Subject Request Procedures

### 4.1 Submission
- Requests submitted via email to privacy@fathuss.com
- Include: Full name, email, and specific request details
- Identity verification required

### 4.2 Response Time
- Acknowledgment within 1 business day
- Fulfillment within 30 days (extendable by 60 days if complex)

### 4.3 Fees
- No fees for standard requests
- Reasonable fees may apply for excessive or unfounded requests

## 5. Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)

### 5.1 High-Risk Processing
- Code execution and analysis
- Cross-border data transfers
- Automated decision-making

### 5.2 DPIA Requirements
- Conducted for high-risk processing activities
- Reviewed annually or when significant changes occur

## 6. Data Breach Notification

### 6.1 Internal Procedures
- Breaches reported to Data Protection Officer within 24 hours
- Investigation and containment within 72 hours

### 6.2 External Notification
- Supervisory authority notified within 72 hours for high-risk breaches
- Affected users notified without undue delay

## 7. International Data Transfers

### 7.1 Adequacy Decisions
- Transfers to countries with EU adequacy status allowed
- Standard Contractual Clauses used for other transfers

### 7.2 Transfer Mechanisms
- Binding Corporate Rules for intra-group transfers
- Approved certification mechanisms

## 8. Data Protection by Design and Default

### 8.1 Privacy by Design
- Data minimization principles applied
- Privacy settings enabled by default
- Regular privacy impact assessments

### 8.2 Technical Measures
- Encryption of personal data in transit and at rest
- Pseudonymization where possible
- Access controls and audit logging

## 9. Cookies and Tracking

### 9.1 Cookie Policy
- Essential cookies for platform functionality
- Analytics cookies with user consent
- Marketing cookies with explicit consent

### 9.2 Consent Management
- Clear cookie banner with granular controls
- Easy withdrawal of consent

## 10. Training and Awareness

- Annual GDPR training for all staff
- Regular updates on privacy best practices
- Incident response training

## 11. Audit and Accountability

- Annual compliance audits
- Data processing records maintained
- Regular reporting to management
